About

GuitaShop.com MUSIC IS THE RYTHM OF LIFE Thank you for dropping by, and for your interest in the history of GUITAR SHOP . My name is Hamouda Mdemagh, and I have been the managing Director of GUITAR SHOP since 2011. The company was founded by my father HABIB, senior, in 1991. From the time I was 12 years old, he made me part of the business step by step. For the Mdemagh family, music has always been a passion and a central pursuit in our lives. I am originally a Bass player and a string instrument maker by training. I was quite young when my father put me in charge of the company, and he gave me a lot of invaluable advice to guide me along the way. One of the things he told me and that has proven the most valuable is to always listen to the customers, and learn from them. Even though the world has changed so much since then, our motto is and will remain: Our customers determine our decisions.
